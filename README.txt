FLYING TIME TWITTER BOT

This is my first project featuring Python3 and Twitter APIs.
It was also made with educational purposes.

INDEX

TFB.py                  - Main routine.
TFB_CALCULUS.py         - Calculations of necessary numbers related to dates, days, hours, minutes, etc. As well as conversions needed.
TFB_PROGRESSBAR.py      - Representation of the progressbar.
TFB_MILESTONES.py       - Representation of some of the representative moments in the year progress.
TFB_FUNFACTS.py         - Representation of some interesting numbers, like minutes left in a year. (Currently not used)
